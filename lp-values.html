Lead Like The Savior

  • Show respect to all people
  • Minister wit love and kindness to others
  • Show personal courage and stands up for what is right
  • Balance giving admonition and nurturing
  • Be trustworthy and show integrity of heart
  • Strive for increased levels of spiritual strength

Act Under the Direction of the Spirit

  • Spiritually prepare yourself for work
  • Seek to know the will of the Lord for your work
  • Apply doctrine to your work
  • Make inspired decisions
  • Act upon the knowledge you have, even if the overall view isn't fully understood

Align With the Brethren

  • Understand the purposes of the Brethren as they relate to the work
  • Consider what the world can offer, but grow the Church after the Lord's pattern
  • Present the truth and best thinking to your leaders
  • Work to build the Church, not just your professional discipline
  • Consider what is best for the global church

Define Direction

  • Anticipate the needs of those you serve
  • Envision what the future looks like and articulate it
  • Articulate clearly how to achieve the defined vision
  • Know that what you are doing is right for the Church
  • Be willing to change direction when needed

Counsel Together

  • Invite input from and sincerely listens to others
  • Focus on asking the right questions
  • Speak up and shares your perspective
  • Be receptive and humble when receiving counsel
  • Put the interests of the council above personal interests
  • Involvs others across the organization in your work

Build Capability

  • Lead others to become better than they knew they could become
  • Help yourself and others improve upon and gain new talents
  • Stretch yourself and others to achieve greater results
  • Help yourself and others learn from their experiences
  • Improve yourself and others by openly seeking and sharing the truth

Organize the Work

  • Use data to plan the work more effectively
  • Identifie your most important work
  • Create meaningful plans
  • Enable work through proper structure, processes, and tools
  • Clearly define accountabilities and how performance is measured

Accomplish the Work

  • Lead meaningful change
  • Match people's strengths to the work that needs to be done
  • Take the initiative to act and enables others to act for themselves
  • Quickly identify solutions to challenges
  • Use wisdom in spending the widow's mite
  • Get results

Render an Account

  • Render an account for performance regularly
  • Help others continuously progress
  • Represent the truth clearly and accurately
  • Hold yourself and others accountable
  • Express appreciation for accomplishments
